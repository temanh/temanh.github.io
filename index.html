What is Mental Health?



Mental health defines your current emotional, social and
psychological well being. It has an tremendous effect on
a persons ability to maintain healthy relationships, make
proper choices, and sustain a sound lifestlye. Ensuring
we are taking time to enjoy the things we love, and nourish
meaningful relationships is critical to conserve mental
wellness.

Warning Signs



Understanding warning signs is invaluable tool that can
potentially save you or someone you love. Each person deals
with deteriorating mental health differently but just being
cognizant of the common symptoms could identify decline
at an early stage.



  • Withdrawal from friends and family

  • Loss of interest in things you used to enjoy

  • High and low extremes of emotion

  • Disorganized or confused thoughts

  • Thinking or talking about suicide

Seeking Help?

Mental Health Counselors

These professionals specialize in group and individual
settings tailoring to specific needs. They implement their
practice out of various facilites, including; hospitals,
privately owned clinics and educational instutions. If you
are struggling with anxiety, depresssion, trauma or other
mental health illnesses please click below for more information.
National Institute of Mental Health

Psychologists

These professionals evaluate and treat mental illnesses.
By studying normal and abnormal mental states, they
can properly evaluate the current condition of their
patients through multiple sessions. Psychologists are
more likely to work with patients with severe mental
conditions. Usually they are also higher qualified than
mental health counselors. See below for more information.
Various Kinds of Psychologists

Good Habits

Diet

This is one of the most important factors for nourishing
your mental state. Understanding proper nutrition can
positively impact your lifestyle and cognitive development.




Exercise

Exercise is not only for your physical health. It plays
a major role in fueling your cognitive abilites. Without
regular exercise your mental state will begin to see a decline.




Sleep

There is a very close relationship between the amont of
sleep you are getting and your mental state. The duration and
frequency of quality sleep will make you energized to take on
the day.
